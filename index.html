Stardew Dino Valley

🌾 Restore an ancient community center greatly inspired by Stardew Valley. 🦖 Unearth fossils, and raise dinosaurs. 🌱 Experience farming, archaeology, and skyblock survival elements in one epic adventure!

Explore the Valley

🌟 Core Features

🌿

Skyblock Survival with Prehistoric Challenges

Transform skyblock with Ex Nihilo: Sequentia resource generation. Create unique island challenges while building your prehistoric paradise with automated systems and advanced tools.

🦖

Fossils & Dinosaur Revival

Excavate fossils, extract DNA, and breed dinosaurs using Fossils and Archeology Revival. Build enclosures, care for prehistoric creatures, and unlock the secrets of a lost world.

🌾

Farming & Fishing with Stardew Progression

Experience bundle-based progression with 58+ ground crops and 26+ tree crops from Croptopia. Dynamic seasons affect your farming while you work toward community restoration.

🗓️

Dynamic Seasons & Calendar

Live through 28-day seasons with Serene Seasons. Watch your world transform with weather effects, seasonal crops, and festival celebrations throughout the year.

💕

Relationships & Villagers

Build meaningful relationships with MCA Reborn's heart-based system. Give gifts, get married, start families, and become part of the community with 0-10 heart progression.

🐅

Unique Wildlife & Creatures

Encounter realistic and mythical wildlife with Alex's Mobs and Better Animals Plus. Build thriving ecosystems filled with both prehistoric and modern creatures.

🎯 Progression System

1

🏗️ Build Pelican Town

Create the town center, Pierre's shop, Gus's saloon, and essential buildings. Set up your community foundation.

2

🌱 Start Farming

Plant seasonal crops, set up fishing spots, and begin gathering resources for your first bundles.

3

📋 Complete Bundles

Turn in items to restore the Community Center room by room, unlocking new areas and abilities.

4

🦖 Discover Fossils

Excavate ancient remains, extract DNA, and begin your journey into prehistoric creature revival.

⚖️ Choose Your Path

Will you restore the Community Center through dedication and hard work, or take the corporate route with JojaMart?

🏛️ Community Center Route

The Traditional Path

  • 🥬 Pantry Bundle → Unlock Greenhouse
  • 🎣 Fish Tank Bundle → Bridge to Desert
  • ⛏️ Boiler Room Bundle → Mine Cart System
  • 🪵 Crafts Room Bundle → Secret Woods Access
  • 💰 Vault Bundle → Bus to Desert/Quarry

Restore the heart of the community through dedication and friendship.

🏢 JojaMart Route

The Corporate Path

  • 💳 Pay 35,000 emeralds for Greenhouse
  • 💳 Pay 25,000 emeralds for Bridge
  • 💳 Pay 15,000 emeralds for Mine Carts
  • 💳 Pay 40,000 emeralds for Bus
  • 🏪 Convert Community Center to Warehouse

Fast-track your progress with cold, hard emeralds.

🦎 Wildlife & Creatures

🦖 Prehistoric Dinosaurs

Revive ancient creatures from fossils. Care for T-Rex, Triceratops, Velociraptors, and more. Build sanctuaries and manage breeding programs.

🐺 Alex's Mobs Wildlife

Encounter realistic animals like bears, wolves, eagles, and exotic creatures. Each with unique behaviors and interactions.

🐟 Aquaculture Fish

90+ fish species across different biomes. Upgrade from wooden rods to neptunium gear. Master tackle boxes and bait systems.

🦅 Better Animals Plus

Enhanced animal behaviors and new species. Build ecosystems that feel alive and dynamic with improved AI.

🌲 Twilight Forest Bosses

Structured adventure progression through mystical bosses. From Naga to Ur-Ghast, each victory unlocks new areas.

🏚️ Enhanced Dungeons

Explore improved mineshafts and dungeons with YUNG's mods. Discover rare loot and face challenging encounters.
